Recruitment day

Join Huawei to have a brilliant future!

Globalization is the main strategy of Huawei for the next 3-5 years. To achieve this goal, Huawei needs to train the talents for the future leaders.

So if you are ambitious, optimistic and are eager to have a successful in your professional life, don't hesitate to apply for the jobs of Huawei.

The recruitment day will be held on December 8 at Télécom ParisTech.

Amphi B310
Télécom ParisTech
46 rue Barrault
75013 Paris

Agenda:

  • Presentation of Huawei
  • Interview
  • Cocktail

Job opportunities

The vacant jobs are presented on the job opportunities page. Please do not hesitate to send us your CV and cover letter using the form at the bottom of each opportunity. Once your application is accepted, you will be invited for an interview at the recruitment day.

Huawei offers are divided into three job categories:

  • Client Manager
  • Solution Manager
  • Customer Reception Manager
